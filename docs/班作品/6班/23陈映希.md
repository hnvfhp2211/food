<center><span style="font-size:52px"> 鱼香茄子</center>

鱼香茄子的来历：鱼香茄子是一道川菜经典菜肴，外文名称为Eggplants with Garlic Sauce，属鱼香味型代表菜，起源于四川省达州市达川区。

成菜特征
鱼香茄子是中国八大菜系中川菜的著名菜肴，其味道鲜美，柔软润香，鲜咸适口，营养丰富。

食材原料  
长茄子3个
猪肉馅适量
蒜适量
小红辣椒适量
豆瓣酱1大勺
盐1小勺
糖2勺
醋4勺
生抽2勺
鸡精1小勺
香油适量
玉米淀粉适量 


**制作方法**


制作步骤
步骤
具体操作

步骤1
准备食材

![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/ca1349540923dd54564eaa638045a4de9c82d158e22e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤2

将茄子切成约七八厘米的长条、蒜切末、小红辣椒切段、豆瓣酱剁碎备用。

![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/18d8bc3eb13533fa828be325f99fea1f4134970aed2e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤3

往茄条里撒少许盐，然后拌匀。让其腌制10分钟左右。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/241f95cad1c8a786c9172f053645de3d70cf3bc7fa2e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤4

茄子腌好后，将盆里多余的水去掉。然后撒适量的干玉米淀粉拌匀备用。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/c83d70cf3bc79f3df8dc863bebedda11728b4710832e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤5

取一只干净的小碗，然后依次放入玉米淀粉、糖、鸡精、生抽、醋，再倒入半碗清水，调成碗汁备用。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/bba1cd11728b4710b91295c79282d4fdfc039245852e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤6

锅中加入适量的油，油热后将茄条依次放入锅中煎炸至表面微黄后盛出备用。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/c9fcc3cec3fdfc039245b4b385739094a4c27d1e882e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤7

另换一只锅，锅中加入少许底油，然后将肉馅倒入锅中煸炒。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/1ad5ad6eddc451da81cb57e0e7b14566d0160924942e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤8

煸炒至肉馅变白成熟，加入豆瓣酱、小红辣椒煸炒匀均。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/b7fd5266d0160924ab18e310854b22fae6cd7b899e2e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤9

加入蒜末煸炒出香味。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/aa18972bd40735fae6cd03b2cf1d18b30f2442a7a22e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤10

将茄条放入锅中煸炒。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/7a899e510fb30f2442a7a40899d9c643ad4bd113a52e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)

步骤11

将碗汁倒入锅中，大火烧开后翻炒均匀。然后关火淋少许香油即可装盘。![](https://bkimg.cdn.bcebos.com/pic/d043ad4bd11373f08202de94f5435cfbfbedab64ae2e?x-bce-process=image/format,f_auto/watermark,image_d2F0ZXIvYmFpa2UyNzI,g_7,xp_5,yp_5,P_20/resize,m_lfit,limit_1,h_1080)
